We are on a crusade to make the youth sports experience better for coaches, parents and MOST IMPORTANTLY, our kids (worth mentioning that we are parents and coaches too).

​ Did you know that about 1 in 1,000 kids earns a college sports scholarship yet nearly half of parents think their kids have scholarship potential?

No wonder it’s like a pressure cooker on the sidelines and in the bleachers!
​

So we are building technology for your mobile device to help coaches and parents communicate in a better way and create a better sports experience for our kids. ​

WE CAN HELP

Our mobile technology platform is secure and private

Our objective age- and gender-specific standards, developed by experts, will align coaches and parents expectations and goals from the beginning of the season.
Our fair assessment tools will help coaches and parents objectively assess their kids performance and help improve their development.
​
Our communciation tool will create a quality feedback loop between parents and coaches to make sure kids are having fun and learning.​
Our tryout tools will bring objectivity and fairness to a relatively chaotic and painful experience for coaches, parents and kids.​
